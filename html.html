Frequently asked questions


Who is BoardCollective for?

BoardCollective is designed for board members of all kinds, from aspiring members to current members; from for-profits to non-profits and everything in between.

I’m not a board member currently. Why should I join BoardCollective?

Whether you’re just getting started on your board journey or in transition, BoardCollective provides critical learning opportunities and insights to make you a more knowledgeable board member and your candidacy more competitive.

What makes BoardCollective different from a corporate directors' association?

BoardCollective is a first-of-its-kind sustainability and ESG learning platform, designed specifically for board members.

Can I get credit for learning hours?

Yes. BoardCollective certifies all learning hours associated with the trainings it offers.

How is BoardCollective different from other learning platforms?

BoardCollective is not a “box ticking” learning platform. We are committed to helping board members learn and grow through innovative learning approaches and practical, real-world examples and peer learning.
